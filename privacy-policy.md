# Privacy Policy for Vocabify

**Last updated:** March 7, 2026

## What Vocabify Does

Vocabify is a Chrome extension that helps users learn vocabulary by translating words they select while browsing and scheduling spaced-repetition reviews.

## Data We Collect

- **Saved vocabulary words**: Words and phrases you explicitly choose to save, along with their translations and review progress.
- **Learning statistics**: Review scores, streak counts, and study session data to power your learning insights.
- **Device identifier**: A randomly generated ID used to associate your data with your device. This is not linked to your identity.

## How Data Is Stored

- Vocabulary and learning data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and synced to our backend (Convex) to enable features like spaced repetition and learning insights.
- No account or login is required.

## External Services

When you select a word to translate, the selected word is sent to the following third-party APIs solely to provide translations, definitions, synonyms, and antonyms:

- Google Translate (translate.googleapis.com)
- MyMemory Translation API (api.mymemory.translated.net)
- Datamuse API (api.datamuse.com)
- Free Dictionary API (api.dictionaryapi.dev)

Only the specific word or phrase you select is sent. No other browsing data, page content, or personal information is transmitted.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
- No browsing history or page content
- No health, financial, or location data
- No authentication credentials

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with third parties. Data sent to translation APIs is used solely to return translations and is not stored by us beyond caching to improve performance.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your saved vocabulary and learning data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome. This removes all locally stored data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
